Taco Meat

Description

Easy to make Taco dish

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon garlic salt

Steps

  1. Step 1 Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
  2. Step 2 Season beef with onion powder, garlic salt, celery salt, and cumin. Pour tomato sauce over the beef, stir to coat, and simmer until thickened, slightly, about 5 minutes.
